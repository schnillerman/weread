<i>Weread</i> is a web content read-it-later app that allows users to save articles, blog posts, and other web content for a better reading experience.

<i>Weread</i> is and will be free to download and use. As it's open source and under a free license, you can even take the code and modify it!

* <b>Android:</b> Ready to use native app.
* <b>Free to Use:</b> As always, this app is free to download and use!
* <b>Open Source:</b> Since this app is MIT licensed, you can modified it!
